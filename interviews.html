How to Ace Your Next Interview

Overview of Interviews

What to expect and what to be aware of during the interview process.

Typical Interview Format

  • Introductions
  • Interviewer background
  • Discussion and questions
  • Your questions for the interviewer
  • Closing discussion (timeline and next steps)
  • Thank you and follow-up

What You Need to Be Aware Of

  • Know your goals and what you are looking for in a role
  • Professional communication and confident body language are important
  • First impressions matter and decisions may be made early
  • Interviewers may bring unconscious biases into the interview

How Should I Answer Interview Questions?

The STAR method is a commonly used approach for answering behavioral interview questions.
