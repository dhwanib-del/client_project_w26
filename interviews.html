Overview of Interviews

What to expect & be aware of

Typical Interview Format

  • Introduction
  • Interviewer background
  • Discussion (Q&A)
  • Questions you want to ask
  • The close (timeline & next steps)
  • Thank you/follow-up

What You Need to Be Aware Of

  • You need to know what you want
  • Confident and assertive behavior is valued by U.S. employers
  • Decisions are often made early
  • Interviewers may bring biases into the interview
